About Us

Bakone People Empowerment was established in 2020 for the sole purpose of empowering people from disadvantaged areas, as well as women, men, and youth around the country irrespective of their background.

Our Mission

  • Empower young children and youth with better knowledge and understanding of the educational systems and social interactions through community outreach programs.
  • Raise awareness that will create opportunities for them to unlock their future and make informed decisions for youth and parents through effective mentoring and public workshops.

Our Projects

  • Youth Mentorship Program - Guiding youth through career choices and life skills training.
  • Community Literacy Initiative - Promoting reading and writing among children in rural areas.
  • Women's Empowerment Workshops - Supporting women with entrepreneurial skills and resources.
